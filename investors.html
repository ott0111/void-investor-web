Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment varies by opportunity. Our current funding round has a minimum of $100,000, while sponsorship opportunities start at $25,000.

How often do you provide investor updates?

We provide quarterly detailed financial reports, monthly performance updates, and immediate notifications for significant events or milestones.

What are the exit opportunities?

Potential exit strategies include strategic acquisition by larger gaming companies, IPO, or secondary market sales. We project a 5-7 year horizon for optimal returns.

How do you manage risk?

We mitigate risk through diversified revenue streams, strong team contracts, insurance coverage, and conservative financial management with focus on sustainable growth.
