How can I join Void Esports?

We hold regular tryouts and scouting events. Follow our social media for announcements, or apply through our academy program.

What games do you compete in?

We currently compete in CS:GO, Valorant, League of Legends, Overwatch 2, and Rocket League. We're always expanding to new titles.

Do you offer sponsorships?

Yes, we offer various sponsorship packages. Contact our business team for detailed information about partnership opportunities.

How can I watch tournaments?

All our matches are streamed on Twitch and YouTube. Follow our channels for notifications about upcoming matches and events.
